Nencoin or NEC

Virtual energy coin.

Nencoin History

Nencoin was created on Nov, 2025 under solana network. Since Nencoin is a meme coin product, the developer considered that Nencoin is not only a meme coin but a digital coin to use for daily transaction by the people in virtual world as their asset to keep or to use it on trading. Nencoin can hold for a long time and there is a rewards for those who have it in future.

How to get Nencoin?

  • ->Create a solana Wallet.
  • ->Put the contract address of Nencoin.
  • ->Trade or Exchange your Solana to Nencoin.

What is the main idea of Nencoin?

Nencoin (NEC) is a meme coin designed to act as money and as a form of payment under Solana Network.
